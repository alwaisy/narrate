Most indie hackers think they have a product problem. They build a great tool, get traffic, but see zero signups. The hard truth is, you probably have a landing page problem. Your visitors don't care about the technology behind your app. They care about what it does for them. If your headline reads like a feature list, you're losing people in seconds. And if you expect them to pull out a credit card without actually seeing the product in action, you're asking for too much trust upfront. Stop trying to sound smart and start showing exactly how you solve their problem.
