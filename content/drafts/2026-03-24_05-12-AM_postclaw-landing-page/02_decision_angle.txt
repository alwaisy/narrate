This founder went from zero signups to 5 paying users and $150 MRR in ten days by making two specific decisions. First, they deleted a headline that explained the product ("Publish on 13 platforms from one chat") and replaced it with a pure outcome ("Your social media. Done in 30 seconds"). Signups jumped immediately. Second, they realized signups aren't sales. So they recorded a raw, unedited 30-second screen capture of themselves actually using the tool. Within 48 hours, they had paying customers. Two micro-decisions that turned traffic into revenue.
