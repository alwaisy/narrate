Launching a product and hearing complete silence is brutal. You spend weeks building, you finally share it, and... nothing. No signups for an entire week. It's easy to assume the market doesn't want what you built and just quit. But sometimes the difference between failing and getting your first 5 paying users is just a few words on a screen and a 30-second video. It's a reminder that building the tech is only the first step. You have to learn how to speak your customer's language.
