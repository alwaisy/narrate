If I had to start from 0 all over again…

These are the 6 insights I’d build around 👇

 1. People don't buy features, they buy a faster way to get things done.
 2. If your headline describes the tech, you're losing signups.
 3. If your headline describes the outcome, you win.
 4. "Your social media. Done in 30 seconds" beats "Publish on 13 platforms from one chat" every time.
 5. Signups do not equal sales. You need to show, not just tell.
 6. A raw, unedited 30-second screen recording converts better than a slick, scripted demo.
