My biggest lesson on indie products:

When you spot a founder building for their own pain point, watch closely.

Study the execution, make them explain the real problem.

A wise builder said: 
Marketing software is not like marketing a local service business.

He was right.

But here is what he did not say:

If you assume B2C users will pay $10, you waste money on ads.
Then your conversions flatline.
Then your entire project stalls out.

One B2B team license is 10x better than a single B2C subscriber.
And 100x better than a user expecting free content.

Target your audience accordingly.

The best B2B customers are not expensive.
Losing time on the wrong market is.

Shift your focus to B2B and grow real revenue.
