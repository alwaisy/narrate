3 weeks ago the founder of PostClaw had 0 revenue. Today they hit $150 MRR.

(The pivot was incredibly simple)

One moment they were:
- Launching an AI scheduling tool.
- Focusing on tech features.
- Seeing zero signups for a full week.
- Explaining how the product worked.

But today:

- They changed one sentence to focus on the result.
- They added a raw unedited 30-second product demo.
- They stopped talking and started showing.

A lot can happen in less than 48 hours.
(Just simplify the message)

The lesson here is clear for any solo builder.

Explanation leaves room for doubt. Demonstration closes it.
