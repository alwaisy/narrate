## HummingDeck

Share documents with tracking. Built-in digital sales rooms.

[Visit Website](https://hummingdeck.com/?ref=microlaunch)

[Marketing & Growth](https://microlaunch.net/category/marketing)Saas Subscription

**HummingDeck** lets you share proposals, decks, and documents via tracked links. See who opened what, which pages they read, and how long they spent. Get notified the moment someone views your content. Group documents into digital sales rooms with tabs, embedded videos, forms, and mutual action plans. Three-layer bot detection filters out email scanners so every view is a real person. Free plan available.
