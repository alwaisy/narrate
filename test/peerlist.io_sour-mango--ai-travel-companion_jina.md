**Travel smarter and live anywhere** with Sour Mango, the all-in-one app designed for digital nomads. This innovative app combines destinations, visas, AI itineraries, food, stays, and community features into one seamless experience. _Plan trips in seconds_ with AI-powered itineraries that offer multi-city routes, local gems, and budget-aware options. Chat with your personal AI travel assistant for destination tips, packing advice, and troubleshooting travel issues.

*   **Discover your next base**: Browse cities ranked by Wi-Fi speed, cost of living, safety, and nomad community.

*   **Visa requirements**: Instantly view visa rules for 195+ countries with color-coded results.

*   **Stay legal**: Track all your visas in one place and receive alerts before expiry dates.

*   **Work ready**: Test Wi-Fi speeds at cafes, coworking spaces, and hotels to ensure connectivity for work.

*   **Fair pricing**: Use AI to check real local prices and negotiate with confidence.

*   **Community connection**: Meet fellow nomads, share tips, and find coworking buddies.

*   **Essential tools**: Real-time currency conversion and translation at your fingertips.

Built by a solo nomad, Sour Mango is available on iOS and Android, offering a free start with premium features through the Ripe Membership. Join the waitlist for early access and explore the world without hassle.
