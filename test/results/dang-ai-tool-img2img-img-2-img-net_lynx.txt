What is the main use case for img2img.net?

   ​

   The main use case for Img2Img.net is to simplify the AI image-to-image
   (img2img) transformation process. It allows users to upload their own
   photos and instantly apply various artistic styles—such as Studio
   Ghibli, Cyberpunk, or Oil Painting—without needing to write complex
   prompts or understand technical AI parameters. Itʼs perfect for
   creating unique social media avatars, artistic portraits, and creative
   visual content.

   ​

Who is the target audience of img-2-img.net?

   ​

   Our target audience includes social media creators, digital artists,
   hobbyists, and anyone who wants to explore the power of AI art but
   finds existing tools like Midjourney or Stable Diffusion too complex.
   It is also a valuable resource for designers looking for quick style
   inspiration or individuals wanting to personalize their digital
   presence with high-quality, AI-generated visuals.

   ​

What model of AI does img2img.net use?

   ​

   Img2Img.net leverages advanced Stable Diffusion models optimized for
   image-to-image tasks. We use a combination of fine-tuned checkpoints
   and ControlNet technology to ensure that the AI maintains the
   structural integrity of the original photo while accurately applying
   the chosen artistic style, resulting in high-fidelity and aesthetically
   pleasing transformations.
