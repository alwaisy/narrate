**Glancelytics** is a privacy-first website analytics tool built for
teams that want clear traffic insights without cookies, invasive
profiling, or GA4 complexity.

Track the metrics that matter in a clean dashboard:

-   pageviews

-   unique visitors

-   top pages

-   referrers

-   devices

-   live activity

Glancelytics is designed to stay simple:

-   no cookie consent banner for analytics

-   no personal data collection

-   lightweight script

-   fast setup with one script snippet

-   free plan available for one site

It works well for founders, indie makers, agencies, and product teams
that want a calmer way to understand website traffic without the noise
of a full marketing stack
